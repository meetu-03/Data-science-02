# What is Statistics?

## Definition

Statistics is the branch of mathematics that deals with collecting, organizing, analyzing, interpreting, and presenting data to make informed decisions.

It helps us understand patterns, trends, and relationships in data.

## Example

Suppose a teacher wants to know the average marks of 5 students.

| Student | Marks |
|----------|-------|
| Rahul | 80 |
| Priya | 90 |
| Amit | 70 |
| Neha | 85 |
| Karan | 75 |

### Calculation of Average

```text
Average = (80 + 90 + 70 + 85 + 75) / 5
Average = 400 / 5
Average = 80
```

So, the average marks of the class are **80**.
